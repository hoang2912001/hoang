Grooveling around an empty crater in the middle of L Corp's decrepit Nest, Zena of the 'Arbiter of the Head' exclaims apperant frustration at the sudden disappearance of a so called 'The Library'. "The Claw, The Eye and the Head are trying their best to-" Said Zena, stuttering over the splutter of a crystaline-like object lodged in her chest. "-Excuse me, have been dealing with an excessive threat called the 'Library' which threatened the existence of the City itself. However unfortunate the disappearance of this institution is, we are proud as one of the Wings of the city to expel them." She finished, before disappearing and ghosting our reporters on the spot. Citizens inside the City are now in an uproar, and are demanding better public infrastructure systems.
